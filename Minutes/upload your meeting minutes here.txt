        A clean, intuitive user experience.
        A scalable architecture that could support future development.

Meeting 2 - Design Review & Technical Direction
Date: 13th November 2025
Attendees:
    Client: Henry Singer
    Team: Josh Pinder, Alex Banfield, Roman Grixti, Alex McCormick
Purpose:
    To review early design work, present technical decisions, and gather client feedback.

Discussion Summary:
    The team presented initial wireframes and mockups created using Canva.
    Navigation flow and layout were explained, including:
        Bottom navigation bar structure.
        Discovery and connection screens.
    Feedback from Henry led to:
        Minor changes to the navigation bar layout to improve usability and clarity.
    The team presented findings from their framework research, explaining:
        The decision to use React with Expo for frontend development.
        The choice of Supabase for backend services, including authentication and database management.
    These technologies were selected due to:
        Rapid development capabilities.
        Scalability.
        Strong support for authentication and real-time features.

Key Outcomes:
    Henry approved:
        The overall UI design direction.
        The proposed frontend and backend technology stack.
    Agreement on a clear development roadmap moving forward.

Actions Agreed:
    Team to:
        Update wireframes based on feedback.
        Update navigation-bar based on feedback.
        Begin implementing authentication functionality.
        Start development of the discovery feature.
